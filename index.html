Our Story

It all started in 2017 at a Value Chain Seminar on Agribusiness and eCommerce anchored by our Founder, Adama J. Adama. We started with just a 100 hectares farmlands and a handful of tractors. Now we have access to over 254, 000 hectares farmlands in Osun,Ogun, Oyo, Ondo, Ekiti, Ondo, Delta, Benue, Niger, Jigawa, Kano, Kaduna, Katsina States and still counting. And have aggregated over 3000 tractors and other farm equipment. Within 9 months, we moved from shared office space to our fully owned office space in a strategic location in Abuja. Google ranked us No. 1 Agritech company in Nigeria. And also No.1 farm equipment rental service in Nigeria.
